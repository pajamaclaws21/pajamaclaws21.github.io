About Me


I'm pajamaclaws21, a 7-or-so-year programmer hailing from the Midwest-est US...est. (I'm a big city fan.) Check out my little guy and my blog.

Recently I read Donna Tartt's masterful novels The Secret History and The Goldfinch. If you're looking for more book recommendations, I loved the Southern Reach Trilogy by Jeff Vandermeer as well as his novel Borne. Fun fact; I've read all of Sailor Moon! I hated it :) Eden II by K. Wroten is my favorite graphic novel I've ever read. I'm not a particular fan of LeGuin, but I thouroughly enjoyed The Lathe of Heaven, and if you're looking for a weird play to read check out Caryl Churchill's Cloud 9 (warning there's sex).

Video game-wise, I'm a big fan of Genshin, Honkai Star Rail (lots of Hoyoverse :sweat:), and Persona 5. I'm super partial to Undertale, didn't like Deltarune as much, and very much want to play Omori. I am a MASSIVE FAN of Hello Charlotte by etherane, if you own a Windows machine go play it now.
