Deconstruction is what ex-evangelicals call their critical analysis of their faith post-exit. I think this is rational. For such a freaky thing, something that screwed us over, how can we decide what we take with us into our daily lives and what to leave behind? How can we acknowledge etc? This is the stance I believe needs to be taken towards the cults. The true nature of what is terrifying is, as i established prior and in my glossary, I find myself on the other side of a mirror from them---as though one rogue thought and boom I'm in. This is the general terror of cults: they lie in the uncanny valley!! Deconstruction is important because for many people the cult contaminates the mainstream culture.
